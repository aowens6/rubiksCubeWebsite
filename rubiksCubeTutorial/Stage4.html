Stage Four: The Top Face

It seems a little counter-intuitive but we’re going to skip the third level for now and solve the top (yellow) face.

In order to get things started you need to have a certain pattern of yellow cubits on top called the “L”. It is suggested to read all of these directions before you start.

There are two easy algorithms called "Double Twins" and "The Spinster" used only in this step, so don’t get confused.

Double Twins

  1. Right side UP
  2. Top to the left
  3. BACK side counter clockwise
    Hint: at this point you will have two columns with two white cubits each…get it? Double twins!
  4. Top to the right
  5. BACK side clockwise
  6. Right side down

The Spinster

  1. Right side up
  2. Top to the left
  3. Right side down
  4. Top to the left
  5. Right side up
  6. Top to the left 180°
  7. Right side down

You will notice that all that happens is the third level and the yellow face are changing patterns. Keep doing these algorithms in any order UNTIL you get the “L”

It may take several times but once you get the “L”, rotate it to resemble this:

Do “Double Twins” once more and you should get a plus-sign pattern:

Look around the third level and you will find a singular yellow cubit on the row. Orient the cube so the yellow cubit is on your left hand side

Do “The Spinster” algorithm once more and you should get a pattern called “The Fish”

There will be a yellow cubit on either the left:

or the right:

If the yellow cubit is on the right, do “The Spinster”. If it is on the left, you can do “The Other Spinster”:

  1. Left side up
  2. Top to the Right
  3. Left side down
  4. Top to the Right
  5. Left side up
  6. Top to the right TWICE (i.e. 180 degrees)
  7. Left side down

Now the top face should be all yellow!
