Stage Five: The Third Level

There are two algorithms to solve the Third level “Corners” and “Edges”

Corners

  1. Find the side with two corners on the third level that match their colors
  2. Now turn that side to the back
  3. Right side down
  4. Face Clockwise
  5. Right side down again
  6. Back side counter clockwise 180°
  7. Right side up
  8. Face counter clockwise
  9. Right side down
  10. Backside counter clockwise 180 °
  11. Right side down 180 °
  12. Top to the right
Note: You may have to keep doing this algorithm until the corners match their face.

Edges

  1. Face 180 °
  2. Top to the right
  3. Right side down AND Left side down
  4. Face 180 °
  5. Right and left up
  6. Top to the right
  7. Face 180 °
If the edge pieces still aren’t in the right place, keep doing this algorithm until you solve it!
